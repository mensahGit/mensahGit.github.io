Machine Learning...

It's Hot!

"Step Right Up! Step Right Up! Everyone's a winner!" A quote from a 1985, Tom Waits masterpiece abruptly comes to mind. Then, the rage was apparently a little cocaine paired with a late night 'Pasties and a G-String'?

Fast-forward to 2019, and oddly-enough, Machine Learning, Deep Neural Networks and Unsupervised Learning take the cake. My! Times have changed. Or have they? Conceivably, we could write some convoluted algorithm foathed with our favorite loss-function of choice...just to find out if time REALLY has changed. Or we could check the calendar..on that wall over there...suspended with year-old wall putty...gently wavering from the racid fumes of two week old, dirty socks and funky underwear?

Yeah, we could do that. But, who has time to actually 'move' anymore?

Computers do things boring, mudane tasks so much more efficiently.Humanity, as such, has not the time for such idleness.
